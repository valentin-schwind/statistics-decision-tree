Statistical Decision Tree

Explore the statistical decision tree as an interactive web application for teaching, method orientation and early analysis planning in HCI research. The tool is meant as orientation support for students and novice researchers. It does not replace statistical training, supervision or study-specific method consultation.

Swipe horizontally to inspect the full decision tree on smaller screens.
